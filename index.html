ACTIVITIES

Parent Child Dance

The parent child dance is when parent and student come at school and dance. There are food and refreshments and you can meet with your friends. It is super fun and happens in our school auditorium.

Field Day

Field day happens at the end of the school year. Teachers take there students outside to play outside games. A few games are tug of war, trowing water ballons withoute breaking them, and dodgeball.

Grab and Go

Grab and Go's only started in Covid-19 time. It is when I go to school and the teachers are outside and we wave at them.Usally, they PTA gives gifts to and the teachers give school supplies. They usally have themes for each Grab and Go to. One of the themes were 101 dalimations on the 101 day of school.
